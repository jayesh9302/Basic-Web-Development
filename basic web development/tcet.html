1.COMPUTER ENGINEERING

COMPUTER ENGINEERING : Click to visit portal

The department of Computer Engineering, started its journey in the Academic Year 2001-02 and is committed to deliver the program with rigor with active industry participation. Department has 180 seats intake at first year and 20 seats as lateral entry at 2nd year for directly admitted diploma students.The department has Post graduate programme with 18 intake from the Academic Year 2011-12 onwards. The department believes in student centric approach. Its dedicated team of faculty members inculcate relevant knowledge, skills and attitude in students to become successful professionals. The U.G. programme is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi for three years w.e.f. 16.09.2011. UG Programme has been re-accredited for 3 years by NBA w.e.f 1st July 2016. Also the programme is permanently affiliated with UOM since AY 2015-16.

Welcome to Mechanical Engineering at TCET. The Department of Mechanical Engineering started from the academic year 2012-2013 with an intake of 120. Our Department is one of the best among all Engineering colleges in Mumbai University. 6.AIML

AIML : Click to visit portal

A 4-year full time B.Tech Program in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), best suited for college graduates seeking to build class technical experience in the areas of AI, ML and emerging technologies. The program is tailored meticulously with the following objectives: To facilitate effective teaching and practice keeping in mind the industry dynamics and the ever-changing economic scenario. To have major focus in the areas of AI-based Dynamic Modelling, Rich and Explainable Deep learning, Large-scale AI, Environment Understanding and Decision Making, Control Optimization through Reinforcement Learning. To develop learning and the culture of augmenting existing technologies, to meet industry challenges and solutions with State-of-the art infrastructure and latest technology. To collaborate with Industry and Research Laboratories through projects, internships enabling the students to explore, apply various directions of learning. To build timely technical experience amongst learners to meet the pressing demands of the nation in the areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with good placement opportunities in industry and reputed research organizations.
